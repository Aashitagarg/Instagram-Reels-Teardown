# The Reels Activation Gap

**A product teardown on why 130M+ Indian users watch Instagram Reels daily, and almost none of them post one.**

[Read the full deck](./Instagram_Teardown_Aashita_Garg.pdf)

## Problem

Instagram Reels in India has 130M daily active users, averaging 32 minutes per day, with Reels making up 70% of total time spent on the app. Despite this scale, the platform follows the classic 90-9-1 pattern: 90% of users lurk, 9% engage occasionally, and only 1% create regularly.

With 414M Indian users opening Reels multiple times a day and almost none of them posting, the opportunity is clear: convert passive viewers into first-time creators.

## Research

- Reviewed Play Store feedback, Reddit threads, and an existing Kantar/IFSO privacy study to identify recurring complaints
- Conducted 10 user interviews and ran a 52-response survey
- Built three personas from interview data: a 16-year-old who feels his life isn't "post-worthy," a 20-year-old who abandoned a Reel mid-edit due to confusing tools, and a 41-year-old with no motivation to create at all
- Mapped the full user journey from opening the app to posting, identifying exact drop-off points
- Benchmarked Instagram against Snapchat and YouTube Shorts to identify competitive gaps

## Key findings

**Privacy and judgment.** Users are less concerned about strangers online and more concerned about being seen by people they know personally. One in three maintain a separate "finsta" specifically to avoid this.

**Editing friction.** Frequent crashes, audio desync, and a cluttered camera screen with no clear starting point cause users to abandon Reels mid-creation.

**No incentive to post.** Consuming content already satisfies most users. There is no compelling reason for them to create instead of just watch.

## Proposed features

| Feature | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Hide From People I Know** | Post publicly and retain algorithmic reach, while hiding the Reel from your own phone contacts |
| **Templates Hub** | A gallery of ready-made Reel templates with pre-set cuts, transitions, and text |
| **Meta AI Assistant** | Automated caption, hashtag, and edit suggestions at the exact point users abandon a draft |
| **Brand Challenges Hub** | Branded contests with real prizes and visibility, giving users a reason to post beyond algorithmic exposure |

## Estimated impact

Conservative annual estimates, with full assumptions in the appendix of the deck:

| Feature | Estimated annual impact |
|---|---|
| Templates Hub | 1.4M new Reels |
| Meta AI Assistant | 936K additional completed Reels |
| Brand Challenges Hub | 228K first-time creators |
| Hide From People I Know | 25% lift in draft-to-post conversion |

## Success metric

North star metric: percentage of zero-post users who publish their first Reel within 30 days of exposure to one of these features. Supporting metrics include template usage rate, draft-to-post conversion rate, and drop-off rate by journey step.
