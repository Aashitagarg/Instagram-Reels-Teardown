# Instagram Reels: Why 90% Watch, 9% Engage, and Only 1% Create

Instagram has 130 million daily Reels users in India, spending 32 minutes a day, with 70% of their time on the app going to Reels. Almost none of it turns into a Reel of their own. This project set out to figure out why, and what could change it.

[Read the full deck](./Instagram_Teardown_Aashita_Garg.pdf)

## The gap

There's a rough pattern across the internet called the 90-9-1 rule: 90% of users lurk, 9% occasionally engage, and 1% actually create. Instagram in India sits right on that curve. 414 million people open Reels multiple times a day, and almost none of them post.

## Research approach

- Reviewed Play Store feedback, Reddit threads, and an existing Kantar/IFSO privacy study to identify recurring complaints
- Conducted 10 user interviews and ran a 52-response survey
- Built three personas grounded in interview data: a 16-year-old who feels his life isn't "post-worthy," a 20-year-old who abandoned a Reel mid-edit due to confusing tools, and a 41-year-old with no motivation to create at all
- Mapped the full journey from opening the app to posting, identifying the exact points where users drop off
- Benchmarked Instagram against Snapchat and YouTube Shorts to identify competitive gaps

## Core findings

Three barriers came up consistently across interviews and secondary research:

**Privacy and judgment.** Users are less concerned about strangers online and more concerned about being seen by people they know personally. One in three maintain a separate "finsta" specifically to avoid this.

**Editing friction.** Frequent crashes, audio desync, and a cluttered camera screen with no clear starting point cause users to abandon Reels mid-creation.

**No incentive to post.** Consuming content already satisfies most users. There is currently no compelling reason for them to create instead of just watching.

## Proposed features

**Hide From People I Know.** Allows users to post publicly and retain algorithmic reach while hiding the Reel from their own phone contacts. This directly addresses the top privacy concern raised in interviews.

**Templates Hub.** A gallery of ready-made Reel templates with pre-set cuts, transitions, and text, reducing the intimidation of starting from a blank screen.

**Meta AI Assistant.** Automated caption, hashtag, and edit suggestions targeted at the exact point where users abandon a draft.

**Brand Challenges Hub.** Branded contests with real prizes and visibility, giving users a concrete reason to post beyond algorithmic exposure.

## Estimated impact

Conservative annual estimates, with full assumptions in the appendix of the deck:

| Feature | Estimated annual impact |
|---|---|
| Templates Hub | 1.4M new Reels |
| Meta AI Assistant | 936K additional completed Reels |
| Brand Challenges Hub | 228K first-time creators |
| Hide From People I Know | 25% lift in draft-to-post conversion |

## Success metric

North star metric: the percentage of zero-post users who publish their first Reel within 30 days of exposure to one of these features. Supporting metrics include template usage rate, draft-to-post conversion, and drop-off rate by journey step.
